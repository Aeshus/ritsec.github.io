      ## How to Sponsor

      RITSEC is a student organization that operates under Rochester Institute of Technology, a 501(c)(3) organization.

      Sponsorships allow for RITSEC to offer services to its students, fund independent research projects, allow us to host events, and so much more.

      RITSEC accepts both monetary and non-monetary sponsorship, from both individuals and corporations.
