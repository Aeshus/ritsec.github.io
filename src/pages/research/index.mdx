        We encourage all members to share their knowledge. Research presentations are a core part of our weekly meetings and a low-stress way to practice public speaking.

        ### Blog Posts

        We also publish technical blogs, as well as cross-posting selected posts created by our members.

        1.  **Pick a Topic**: Anything tech/security related that you are passionate about.
        2.  **Prepare**: Write a MDX blog post and submit a pull request to the [RITSEC Website](https://github.com/ritsec/ritsec.github.io).
        3.  **Approval**: The Head of Research will review your blog post and give suggestions, and publish.
